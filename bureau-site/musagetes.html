Musagetes

Life without art is no life at all. Musagetes, an international organization whose mission is to bring art to a place of prominence in our lives and communities, understands this well. With strong presence in regions diverse as Canada, Italy and Croatia, Musagetes demonstrates that art can be participatory and socially engaging rather than just something to hang on a museum wall. This effort to connect our societies and art builds stronger communities by establishing a greater sense of belonging.
